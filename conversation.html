Brainstorm English Course Outlines

Our lessons are designed using the highest standard of academically verified materials, and each of our courses offered will include reference material and worksheets based on these books.


All you need to do is bring your pen, a notebook, and you’re ready to learn !


Materials and Lesson Structure

Below are the books we use for each of our courses, and the structure of our lessons and courses in general.


Most books have 10 - 12 units, and 1 class is built around half a unit (1 full unit is covered per 2 classes). How fast you complete the course and progress depends on how many classes you book per week.


So we encourage you to book 2 classes together, to ensure full integration of the subject matters.

  • The first lesson of each course will include placement assessment and signing of a student contract
  • There is a compulsory recap lesson that takes the place of 1 lesson, at the end of each course. This is to ensure full integration of the content has taken place, and to make sure the student is ready to advance to the next level.
